"Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation drives progress by creating new solutions and improving existing ones.

Sustainability

Sustainability ensures resources are used responsibly to meet present and future needs.

Infrastructure

Robust infrastructure underpins economic growth and improves quality of life.

Empowerment

Empowerment fosters self-reliance, confidence, and the ability to achieve goals.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"Innovation is the driving force that shapes our future. It starts with daring to question the status quo and thinking beyond conventional limitations. True innovation goes beyond technology; it focuses on empowering people, especially those at the margins, to improve their lives. Every individual has the potential to innovate, but we must nurture creativity and courage to think differently. By building a world where opportunities are accessible to all, we unleash the power of innovation to transform society. Let us work together, driven by empathy and imagination, to create a future that is inclusive, sustainable, and equitable for all."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Advisor of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
